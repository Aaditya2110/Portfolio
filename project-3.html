Project Overview

Led a comprehensive research and data analysis initiative dedicated to United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation. Leveraging advanced Python techniques, the project involved in-depth data analysis,visualized through graph matrices, ranking assessments, and regression analysis. The primary objective was to extract actionable insights, fostering a deeper understanding of the challenges and opportunities associated with SDG 6. This research not only contributed to the broader discourse on global water and sanitation issues but also provided valuable data-driven solutions to support the ongoing efforts towards achieving the objectives outlined by SDG 6.

This initiative exemplified the intersection of technology and sustainable development, showcasing my proficiency in Python for robust data analysis and visualization.
